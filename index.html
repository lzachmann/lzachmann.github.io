Featured work

A few highlights from some of past and ongoing work

VP / Biomass

Developing a state-of-the-art live aboveground biomass model at 10m resolution. This model leverages a custom Feature Pyramid Network, powered by features extracted Vibrant Planet's foundation model (a Masked AutoEncoder). Input data includes Sentinel-2 imagery, terrain features, and climate variables, enabling accurate and high-resolution biomass and carbon estimates.

VP / Forest structural metrics

Using a host of ML approaches (Bayesian hierarchical allometric models and computer vision) to assign critical structural attributes to individual trees, including size/volume and status (alive or dead) across the western US.

Iggy / Reports

Insights about the demography and amenities (within walking or driving distance) at any address in the US.

Iggy / Data Diligence

A data diligence process, including an interactive mapping application and form to systematically and iteratively validate and improve Iggy's features. The process included a number of aggregate checks (statistical measures, and spatial distributions or spatial consistency of individual features) and spot checks (on individual data points, including ID, location, value).
